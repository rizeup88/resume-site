As a Website & UX/UI designer, you could say, the internet is my place of inspiration, and my devices are my art canvas.

After three years of professional web and UX/UI Design experience, I never seek to stop developing my skills and perspective to change with the times. I especially enjoy designing mobile apps, as well as webpages, but am always open to learning both new forms of Design, as well as new software developed to evolve mobile/desktop design.

I am currently looking for full-time or contract work as a Designer, though I am open to freelance work as well.
